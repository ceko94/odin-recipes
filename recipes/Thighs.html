Homemade Chicken Thighs

A feast for the whole family

Beautiful Chicken thighs are a dish for special occasions where you want to gather family and or friedns together to enjoy it. Believe me

Ingridients

  • One Chicken Thigh per person
  • Spices of your chossing. Recommended are Cavenne pepper, chili, garlic, curry, rosmarin and paprika powder
  • Potatoes
  • Onions
  • Cherry Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Heat up the oven.Marinade the meat with your spices
  2. When the heat reached 180 Degrees Celcius put the meat with a bit of oil or butter in a casserole in the oven
  3. In the meatime chop up only the Potatoes spice them as you wish. When the meat has been in the oven for 30 minutes, you put the potatoes between the chicken thighs
  4. After 20 Minutes you throw the onions and tomatoes on the casserole for the last ten minutes
  5. Now the dish is ready. You can serve it with warm bread like a chiabatta and sauces like aioli or sour cream
  6. The job is done. You can have a remarkable dinner and are beautiful time with your loved ones
